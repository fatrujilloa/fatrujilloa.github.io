Home

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Universität Zürich under the supervision of Corinna Ulcigrai, Professor at Universität Zürich.

I completed a PhD in Mathematics at the IMJ-PRG, Université de Paris, under the joint supervision of Hakan Eliasson, Professor at the Université de Paris, and Bassam Fayad, CNRS Senior Research Scientist at the IMJ-PRG and Professor at the University of Maryland.

Background

  • Since September 2020: Posdoctoral researcher at Universität Zürich.
  • 2018-2020: Temporary teacher and researcher (ATER in French) at Université de Paris.
  • 2016-2020: PhD in Mathematics at IMJ-PRG, Université de Paris.
  • 2013-2015: Master in Mathematics at IMPA (Instituto de Matemática Pura e Aplicada).
  • 2009-2012: Bachelor in Mathematics at Universidad Nacional de Colombia.
You can find a detailed version of my CV here.

Research

My research focuses on Dynamical Systems, particularly in Hamiltonian Dynamics, KAM Theory, Circle Maps and Interval Exchange Transformations.
