Home

I am currently a María de Maeztu postdoctoral fellow at Centre de Recerca Matemàtica (CRM) under the supervision of Marcel Guardia, Professor at University of Barcelona.

I have been recently awarded a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions fellowship (which will start on May 2024) to work at the CRM.

Before I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich under the supervision of Corinna Ulcigrai, Professor at the University of Zurich.

I completed a PhD in Mathematics at the IMJ-PRG, Université de Paris, under the joint supervision of Hakan Eliasson, Professor at the Université de Paris, and Bassam Fayad, CNRS Senior Research Scientist at the IMJ-PRG and Professor at the University of Maryland.

Background

  • 2024-: Posdoctoral researcher at Centre de Recerca Matemàtica, Spain.
  • 2020-2023: Postdoctoral researcher at University of Zurich, Switzerland.
  • 2018-2020: Temporary teacher and researcher (ATER in French) at Université de Paris, France.
  • 2016-2020: PhD in Mathematics at IMJ-PRG, Université de Paris, France.
  • 2013-2015: Master in Mathematics at IMPA (Instituto de Matemática Pura e Aplicada), Brazil.
  • 2009-2012: Bachelor in Mathematics at Universidad Nacional de Colombia.
You can find a detailed version of my CV here.

Research

My research focuses on Dynamical Systems, particularly in Hamiltonian Dynamics, KAM Theory, Circle Maps and Interval Exchange Transformations.
