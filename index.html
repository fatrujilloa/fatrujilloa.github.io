Home

I am currently a Marie Sklodowska-Curie postdoctoral fellow at the Centre de Recerca Matemàtica (CRM), in Spain, through the grant REXHALODYN funded by the European Union under its Horizon Europe - MSCA program.

For a few months in 2024 I was a Maria de Maeztu postdoctoral fellow at the CRM under the supervision of Marcel Guardia, Professor at University of Barcelona. Before this I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich under the supervision of Corinna Ulcigrai, Professor at the University of Zurich.

I completed a PhD in Mathematics at the IMJ-PRG, Université Paris Cité, under the joint supervision of Hakan Eliasson, Professor at the Université de Paris, and Bassam Fayad, CNRS Senior Research Scientist at the IMJ-PRG and Professor at the University of Maryland.

Research

My research focuses on Dynamical Systems, particularly in Hamiltonian Dynamics, KAM Theory, Circle Maps and Interval Exchange Transformations.
